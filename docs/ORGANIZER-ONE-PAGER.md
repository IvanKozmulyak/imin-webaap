## What IMIN Does

IMIN matches solo ticket buyers into **squads of 5** — pre-connected groups who arrive together, chat before the event, and have a built-in social experience.

No extra work for you. No extra cost for attendees. Just **more tickets sold.**

## How It Works

1. **You add IMIN to your ticket flow** — One line of code or a simple widget
2. **Solo buyers opt in** — "Find your squad" checkbox at checkout
3. **IMIN handles the matching** — Algorithmic pairing based on interests, age, vibe
4. **Day-of coordination** — Automated entrance locations, emoji codes, group chat
5. **Post-event feedback** — Ratings system helps you identify great attendees

**Time to integrate:** 30 minutes  
**Ongoing effort:** Zero
